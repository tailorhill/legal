Questions, problems or feedback about QUADRA? We're happy to help.

Contact

Email us at info@tailorhillconsulting.se. We usually reply within a few business days. Please include your device model and what happened — a screenshot helps a lot.

Common questions

I bought the full version but it's locked again.
Open the unlock screen and tap "Restore purchase" (iOS), or simply restart the app while online — your purchase is re-detected automatically.

Does the game need an internet connection?
No — QUADRA plays fully offline. A connection is only needed for the one-time purchase or restoring it.

How do I reset my progress?
Options → RESET ALL PROGRESS. Your purchase is never removed by a reset.
